Great Lakes Science Center

Camp Counselor

As a Camp Counselor at The Great Lakes Science Center, I was responsible for teaching a variety of topics to children through different sessions. These sessions included topics such as forensic analysis, robotic programming, space exploration, and marine biology. The kids in these sessions were given the opportunity to interact with different instruments first hand, design and perform experiments and learn all about the real-world applications of science. As a Counselor, I devised learning outcomes and lesson plans appropriate to the ages of the children.
