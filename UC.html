University of Cincinnati

Smart Walk

Through The University of Cincinnati's Student Government group, a group of developers decided to create a mobile application to guide students to their classes by using the shortest, warmest and most wheel-chair friendly routes on campus. The app was created using HTML, Javascript and CSS. As a member of the development team, I mapped out the locations of various buildings on campus by getting their GPS coordinates. I was also contributed design ideas as well as the name of the application.
