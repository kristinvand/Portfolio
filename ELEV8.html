ELEV8 Incorporated

Designer & Developer

ELEV8 Inc. was founded in Cincinnati, Ohio by a team of 3 students and 2 working professionals. It started with an idea at the AngelHack Cincinnati 2016 Hackathon. Our idea was simply to integrate Artificial Intelligence and voice recognition into education through the power of Amazon's Alexa technology. After winning the competition, we attended Silicon Valley week where we redesigned our approach. We decided that voice recognition shouldn't be limited to those who know how to program but should be available to all content creators. We did this by creating a platform, much like WIX, to allow nonprogrammers the ability to create Alexa skills through a drag and drop interface. Our web application automates the coding process and delivers a simple zip folder with all their content that's ready to upload to their voice recognition enabled technology. This project became a company throughout the process and gained over $40,000 in investment money. Within the company, I had the role of Chief Creative Officer. My contributions included creating company branding such as logos and color palettes. I also was a part of the development that created the web application.
